

# SEO Content Clusters — Deep Research & 20-Page Map

## A. Executive Summary

Quantifier.ai has a solid foundation: 8 blog posts (EN+PL), strong framework pages (NIS2, ISO 27001, SOC 2, GDPR, DORA), 3 compare pages, and the cybersecurity-check quiz. However, the site lacks BOFU content targeting high-intent commercial keywords — the kind that convert visitors into demo requests. The two clusters (NIS2/KSC and SOC 2/ISO 27001/alternatives) represent the highest-value SEO opportunities.

Key findings:
- **NIS2 is a hot topic right now** — KSC law signed Feb 2026, 6-month implementation deadline, ~10,000+ companies affected in Poland alone. SERP competition is MEDIUM — mostly consulting firms and generic tools, few strong vendor pages.
- **SOC 2 / ISO 27001 comparison & alternatives SERPs** are HIGH competition — dominated by Vanta, Drata, Sprinto, Scrut, Scytale. Quantifier is not ranking for any of these terms.
- **Quantifier already has strong blog content** that can be leveraged as cluster pillars: NIS2 directive guide, compliance monitoring guide, continuous compliance article, SOC 2 complete guide.
- **Critical gap**: No "NIS2 compliance software" landing page separate from the framework page (which is more educational). No "ISO 27001 vs SOC 2" comparison content. No evidence collection or audit readiness content.

## C. SERP Research Findings

### Cluster 1 SERPs

**"nis2 compliance software"** — Commercial intent. Dominated by: 3rdRisk (listicle), Heimdal (listicle), Responsum, OneTrust, Complyance, Vanta, Qualys. Competition: MEDIUM-HIGH. Quantifier not ranking. Gap: Most results are either massive enterprise tools (OneTrust, Qualys) or generic listicles. Opportunity: EU-focused, mid-market positioning with AI differentiation. **Recommended: UPDATE `/en/frameworks/nis-2/` to rank for this + NEW dedicated blog "Best NIS2 Compliance Software".**

**"nis2 compliance checklist"** — Informational/Commercial. Dominated by: Device42, DSALTA, Cyrolo, OpsioCloud, Tidal Control. Competition: MEDIUM. Most are generic checklists without tool backing. Gap: No interactive or downloadable checklist from a vendor with actual software. **Recommended: NEW blog article with downloadable checklist.**

**"nis2 readiness assessment"** — Commercial. SoSafe, Advisera (free tool), Glocert (guide), Kiteworks, Thomas Murray. Competition: MEDIUM. Quantifier ALREADY has cybersecurity-check quiz which is essentially this. **Recommended: UPDATE cybersecurity-check SEO metadata + NEW blog article positioning the quiz.**

**"wdrożenie nis2 polska KSC"** — PL, commercial. Dominated by: consulting firms (Resilia, PBSG, Grant Thornton, Trecom), legal news (Clifford Chance, di.com.pl). Competition: MEDIUM. No strong software vendor page in PL. **Recommended: NEW PL blog article about implementation with software.**

**"oprogramowanie do zgodności nis2"** — PL, commercial. Results: dyrektywanis2.pl, Resilia, Wolters Kluwer. Competition: LOW-MEDIUM. Very few software vendors in Polish. **Recommended: UPDATE `/pl/frameworks/nis-2/` meta to target this + supporting blog.**

**"soc 2 automation software"** — Commercial. Dominated by Sprinto, Drata, Scrut, Process Street, Beagle Security listicle, Hicomply, Vanta. Competition: VERY HIGH.
